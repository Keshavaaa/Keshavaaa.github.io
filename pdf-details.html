PDF Assistant - AI Powered Learning Tool

An intelligent learning assistant that transforms static PDF documents into interactive study tools. Inspired by the vision of "books that can talk," this application uses LangChain and Gemini AI to enable conversational learning from any PDF document.

1. Conversational Q&A

Users can ask questions in natural language and receive context-aware answers directly from the PDF content. This feature is powered by a custom-built RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline using LangChain and the Google Gemini API, ensuring accurate and relevant responses.

2. Automated MCQ Generation

To enhance learning and self-assessment, the PDF_Assistant can automatically generate multiple-choice questions based on the document's content. This allows users to test their knowledge and reinforce key concepts in an engaging way.

3. Personalized Study Planning

The application can analyze a document and generate a customized, week-long study plan. This helps users break down complex topics into manageable daily goals, making their study sessions more structured and effective.
